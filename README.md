## 💡 Prompt Engineering Tips
AI models perform better when you provide specific context. Use these strategies within the application:

*   Specify the camera angle: Ask for close-ups or wide shots to add visual variety.
*   Define the tone: Add instructions like "keep it casual" or "use a professional tone."
*   Mention the platform: Tailor your aspect ratio requests for 9:16 vertical video.
*   Include the goal: State if the video intends to educate the viewer or convert a sale.
